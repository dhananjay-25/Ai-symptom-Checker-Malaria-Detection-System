About Us

We are a team of passionate developers and healthcare enthusiasts who aim to bridge the gap between technology and medicine. Our AI-powered solution enables rapid malaria detection using microscopic images of blood smears, eliminating delays and improving healthcare access.

Built using state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), our system identifies whether a patient is infected with malaria or not with high precision. The platform is designed for both users and doctors, providing seamless image uploads, fast results, and the ability to generate professional PDF reports.
